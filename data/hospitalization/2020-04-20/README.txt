These data are daily total hospitalization counts for the Houston and Beaumont MSAs. They were used for estimation of epidemic emergence date, and transmission rate reduction relative to an assumed transmission rate of 0.035 (based on the Austin MSA transmission rate estimate).

On 2020-04-11, the hospital census dropped dramatically. Based on guidance from SETRAC, we replaced that single data point with the total for the previous day. The file name indicate these data are amended and contain this single manual change.
